IELTS

IELTS stands for the International English Language Testing System. It is a standardized test that measures the English language proficiency of non-native English speakers who wish to study or work in countries where English is the primary language of communication. The test assesses the four language skills: listening, reading, writing, and speaking. IELTS is recognized by universities, employers, and governments around the world as a reliable indicator of English language proficiency.
