Aboard Study

Computer

Our computer courses cover basic, diploma, and advanced levels, as well as accounting packages, software, and networking. We also offer computer skill tests to assess your proficiency and provide graphic/web design and PHP/MySQL courses for those interested in web development.

Basic Computer

Basic computer courses are educational programs designed to introduce individuals to fundamental computer concepts and skills. These courses are typically aimed at beginners with little to no prior knowledge of computers. They cover essential topics such as computer hardware and software, operating systems, word processing, spreadsheets, internet basics, email, file management, and basic troubleshooting.

Intermediate Computer

Intermediate computer courses are educational programs designed to build upon the foundational knowledge gained in basic computer courses. These courses are typically aimed at individuals who have completed basic computer training or have some prior knowledge of computers. They cover more advanced topics and skills that are essential for using computers in a professional setting or for more complex tasks.

Abvanced Computer

Learn advanced programming languages like C++, Java, or Python, and explore advanced topics such as data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming.
